# Cognitive Dissonance

> **Definition:**  
> Definition:: The psychological tension or discomfort that occurs when an individual's beliefs or attitudes are inconsistent with their actions or new information. Cognitive dissonance often leads people to change their beliefs or attitudes to reduce the discomfort.
